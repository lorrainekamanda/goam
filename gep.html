Girls Empowerment Program
GEP

Many girls face daily arduous barriers that prevent them from reaching their potential each day. There is increased interest to invest in the girl child due to their vulnerability. Cases of dropping out from schools due to lack of basic sanitary products, early pregnancies and child brides are most common in every other village.
Girls On A Mission Group each month, every term builds on as a driving force to change the girls by providing basic sanitary towels, pantiees and toiletries ensuring that they maintain menstrual hygiene by keeping them in school.
Keeping the girl's progress and a follow through has its key, ensuring a brighter future for every adolescent girl. Our yearly Girl's Empowerment Budget is approximately US$ 5000.

Our mission is to help the most vulnurable in our community and uplift their living standards,we do that through donations.It is often said that People will forget what you said. They will forget what you did. But they will never forget how you made them feel.

History

Girls On A Mission is a Community-Based Organization (CBO) in Kwale County. It was registered on 5th,May 2015 with the mission to source and supply sanitary towels "Our Baby is Born all Glory to God. The mission of this Organization is to demistfy myths that surrounds womanhood. The mision is to source and supply sanitary towels to need school-going girls in Kwale which shall reduce absentism, pregnancies and marriages and inspire the girls to aspire to scale the heights to the completion of their studies. On 3rd, June 2015, we started our first distribution of sanitary towels to Mwakigwere Primary School in Ukunda. The school has 441 girls who benefited from this project."
